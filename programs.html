ICCU, AMCU Care Units



HRS Hospitals, Nandyal houses one of the busiest ICUS in the Nandyal. It comprises of 40 beds of which 19 cater to very sick patients who require multiorgan support, and 21 cater to parents with single organ dysfunction needing constant care and monitoring.

The critical care department at HRS Hospitals Nandyal, under the guidance and leadership of Dr Sachin Chidrawar,treats patients from all specialities like Neurology, Gastroenterology, Pulmonology and Complex surgical cases. It consists of a High Dependency Unit (HDU), Neuro Intensive Care Unit (NICU), Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU) and Intermediate Care Unit (IMCU). Five out of the total 40 beds are cubicles, with strict isolation conditions.

We provide services including Hemodialysis, CRRT, IABP, Mechanical Ventilator Support including both IPPV AND NIV, invasive monitoring including FLOTRAC and IBP monitoring, and ECMO. The department ensures strict aseptic precautions to be followed to prevent the spreading of MDR AND PDR infections in the ICU.
